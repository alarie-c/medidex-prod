About Us

MediDex's vision is to be an incredibly simple and uncomplicated platform to make accessing information about medications easy and simple. Lots of people who didn't grow up with technology understandably have a hard time keeping up with it and navigating the rapidly changing technological climate. Moreover, there are lots of people with disabilities or other accessibility concerns that make navigating the web difficult and time consuming. At MediDex, we hope to relieve these issues and provide a platform that is astonishingly simple to use.
